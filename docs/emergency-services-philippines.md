- Eastern Visayas

---

### Areas NOT Yet Covered by 911

For provinces not yet covered, citizens use alternative numbers:

| Emergency Type | Alternative Numbers |
|----------------|---------------------|
| Police | 117 (Patrol 117 - still active in some areas) |
| Fire | Local BFP station numbers (varies by city/municipality) |
| Ambulance | Local hospital numbers (varies) |
| Barangay | Contact local barangay hall |

## 2. Issues with Current 911 System (per Feb 2026)

Based on user reports and news articles:

- **Calls often go unanswered** - phones ring but no one picks up
- **Transferred repeatedly** - caller passed from operator to operator
- **Coordination issues** - different agencies don't communicate well
- **1/3 are prank calls** - wastes resources
- **Not all LGUs have ambulances** - DILG proposing to procure 300 ambulances

## 3. Emergency Types in the Philippines

### 3.1 Police / Law Enforcement (PNP)

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Crime in Progress | Robbery, Theft, Holdup |
| Violence | Physical assault, Murder |
| Domestic | Domestic violence, Child abuse |
| Traffic | Vehicle accident, Hit and run |
| Public Safety | Bomb threat, Illegal activities |

### 3.2 Fire (BFP - Bureau of Fire Protection)

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Structural | House, building, apartment fire |
| Vehicle | Car, truck, motorcycle fire |
| Environmental | Bush fire, forest fire |
| Utility | Electrical fire, gas leak |
| Hazmat | Chemical spill, flammable materials |

### 3.3 Medical / Ambulance

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Trauma | Accident injury, Fall, Wound |
| Cardiac | Heart attack, Cardiac arrest |
| Respiratory | Difficulty breathing, Choking |
| Medical | Severe illness, Seizure, Diabetic emergency |
| Maternal | Pregnant woman in labor, Complication |
| Poisoning | Food poisoning, Snake bite, Chemical exposure |

### 3.4 Search & Rescue (PCG, MDRRMO)

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Water | Drowning, Capsized vessel |
| Land | Missing person, Lost hiker |
| Collapse | Building collapse, Landslide |

### 3.5 Disaster Response (NDRRMC)

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Natural | Typhoon, Flood, Earthquake, Landslide |
| Evacuation | Need for rescue from disaster area |
